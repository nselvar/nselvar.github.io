R - Apriori - Online Buying Behavior
Ability to recognize and track frequent item sets in data help businesses shift through the layers of seemingly unrelated data for meaningful relationships. Apriori algorithm is one of the most popular data mining approach for finding frequent item sets from a transaction dataset and derive association rules. Rules are the discovered knowledge from the data base. Once frequent item sets are obtained,it is straightforward to generate association rules with confidence larger than or equal to a user specified minimum confidence.In this post we can explore how to find association rules and formulate some predictions for the online store. R - Neural Network & SVM - Predict Success Of Bank Telemarketing
The shift towards decision making (DM) based on machine learning techniques techniques will change the way in which consumer markets and our societies function. Through ML, predictive analytics is being used by businesses to identify these patterns and major trends with the objective to improve the DM and influence future business outcomes. This project proposes an Neural Network (ANN)/ SVM approach to predict the success of telemarketing calls for selling bank long-term deposits. To validate the proposed model, we uses the bank marketing data of 41188 phone calls. The NN attains 90% of accuracy which outperforms other conventional classifiers and confirms that it is credible and valuable approach for telemarketing campaign managers. This machine learning solution examines twitter data for their tonality. The classification, whether a tweet is positive or negative, i.e. the tonality, is done by a deep neural network. The deep convolutional neural network architecture we constructed in this project,is a feedforward neural net using the TensorFlow library in Python.
